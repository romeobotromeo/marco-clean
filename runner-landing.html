Runner FAQ

Who succeeds as a Marco runner?

Operators with hospitality-level polish, logistics instincts, and comfort handling smart home tech. If you can communicate crisply and think two steps ahead, you’ll thrive.

What gear do I need?

A reliable vehicle or e-bike, smartphone, ID for property access, and basic documentation tools. We provide lockboxes, signage, and specialized kits when required.

How does scheduling work?

Mark availability in the app for 4-hour windows. Dispatch balances coverage across hot zones and pre-books runners for surges. You can also claim ad-hoc urgent jobs.

Are there advancement paths?

Yes. High-performing runners graduate into lead roles, vendor management, or full-time ops positions as Marco expands into new neighborhoods.
